About Our School

Building foundations for lifelong learning and character development

🎓 Our School

Established in 2024, Joshi’s First Step School is committed to providing quality education that nurtures young minds and prepares them for a successful future. With experienced teachers and a child-centric approach, we create an environment where learning is enjoyable and effective.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide holistic education that develops students academically, emotionally, and socially while fostering curiosity, creativity, and confidence.

👁️ Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading educational institution that empowers students to become responsible, lifelong learners and positive contributors to society.

💚 Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold honesty and strong moral principles.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do.

Respect

We value diversity and treat everyone with dignity.

Innovation

We encourage creativity and forward thinking.

Collaboration

We work together to achieve common goals.

Responsibility

We take ownership of our actions and commitments.
